The Challenge

Why Legacy Infrastructure Fails

Current timer-based intersections cannot adapt to dynamic city flows, creating cascading gridlock and fatal delays for emergency services.

01
⏱️

Static Timing Inefficiency

Fixed green-light cycles ignore real-time density, leading to empty lanes holding green while cross-traffic builds massive queues.

02
🚑

Emergency Blockage

First responders are trapped behind red lights, directly impacting life-threatening response times by up to 10 minutes.

03
🌊

Cascading Gridlock

A single blocked intersection creates a phantom jam that spreads system-wide without predictive mechanisms to stop it.

04
⚡

High Latency Decisions

Relying on manual operators in control rooms introduces dangerous minutes of delay. Cities need sub-second, autonomous edge processing.
