Our Heritage

A Century of Quiet Mastery

Founded in 1887 in the snow-veiled valleys of Vallée de Joux, Maison Noir was born from a single, uncompromising principle — that a timepiece is not measured by what it shows, but by what it contains.

Each calibre is conceived, decorated and assembled by a single master watchmaker. From the anglage of the bridges to the black-polished steel, every detail is an act of devotion to the craft.
